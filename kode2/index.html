The Kode Network

Decentralized Video Transcoding
developed by Qencode

The problem

Currently market share for web services is concentrated to just a few third-party providers. Companies both big and small are largely dependent on the terms and availability of the few providers available, as well as exposed to risks with privacy and security.

Highly Centralized

Big third-party providers continue consolidate services, gaining even more on their already majority control of marketshare for cloud web services.

Price Descrimination

Providers charge high prices for their services, practicing price discrimination against startups with low volume and creating a barrier of entry to innovators.

Conflict of Interest

Many large third-party providers compete directly with the customers they provide their services to and create a conflict of interest that hurts their clients.

The Solution

The Kode Network is a decentralized video transcoding platform that leverages the latest blockchain technologies, providing the most available, private, secure and cost-effective video transcoding solution for startups and large enterprises alike.

Peer-to-Peer Network

Each transcoding split into smaller parts and distributed across a massive decentralized peer-to-peer network, ensuring cost savings and availability.

Auction Based Bidding

An internal auction is initiated for each task request, driving efficiency innovation and ensuring participants always get the best prices in the market.

Blockchain Technology

Anonymized ledgers provide high levels of privacy across the blockchain, while maintaining an open community and ensuring that the network stays secure.

Why Kode Network?

Privacy

A decentralized transcoding platform that provides a secure and anonymous way to transcode videos.

Availability

A large peer-to-peer network of nodes greatly reduce the chance of the system of being down.

Cost-Savings

Huge efficiency gain by leveraging idle processing power across a distributed network.

Simple UI or API

Provide services without requiring any blockchain or cryptocurrency knowledge.

Robust Video Transcoding

Using Kode Network, customets can convert videos into all the latest containers, formats and codecs, and create to videos with multiple quality and resolution settings.
