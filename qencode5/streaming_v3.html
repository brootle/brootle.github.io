Live Streaming and Transcoding API

Live stream at scale with Qencode

It's simple to launch live encoding and video delivery that will automatically scale with your needs. Our flexible API allows you to customize your streams they way you need them.

Deliver high quality video streams globally

Reach viewers around the world at resolutions up to 4K. As viewership grows, our high-speed globally distributed video delivery infrastructure scales dynamically to provide a seamless user experience in any situation.

Simulcast streams to multiple social video channels or destinations

Live stream your broadcasts simultaneously to multiple destinations, including social video platforms like YouTube, Twitch, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Works with any solution compatible with RTMP, even if your source is WebRTC.
