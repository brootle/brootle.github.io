Digital Rights Management

Protect your content from unauthorized usage

Digital Rights Management systems allow content producers to protect their media through a series of complex encryptions, preventing unauthorized users from watching and sharing your proprietary media.

Qencode has made it easier than ever to safeguard your content by completing integrations with the top DRM providers in the world. With our simple deployment process, getting started with DRM is easier than ever.

Why use DRM?

Creates protection layer to prevent unauthorized use of content

Easily integrated into your transcoding workflow

Prevents piracy by making it difficult to illegally share content

Qencode has fully integrations with the best DRM providers in the world, like Irdeto, BuyDRM, and EZDRM.

How does it work?

1
Your Video File

The original video file is sent to Qencode for encoding and encryption.

2
Encoding

Video file is encoded with the encryption using the exact set of keys.

3
Encryption

Video file is encrypted using protected media keys received from provider.

4
Storage

Encrypted file is stored in your destination location.

5
Playback Requested

Video is requested to be played using an authorized player and session.

6
Authorization

Playback request is authorized by DRM provider and if confirmed, Key is provided and playback begins.

7
Playback Initiated

The video is played back to authorized user seamlessly within milliseconds of being requested.
